Accountancy/Book Keeping

ACCOUNTANCY/BOOK KEEPING

  • Fundamentals of Accounting:Understand the basic principles and concepts of accounting, essential for CUET.
  • Double Entry System:Master the dual aspect of transactions and how to record them correctly.
  • Journal Entries:Learn to accurately record financial transactions in the journal, the first step in bookkeeping.
  • Ledger Posting:Understand how to transfer journal entries to the ledger, creating detailed accounts.
  • Trial Balance Preparation:Learn to compile a trial balance to ensure accuracy in financial records.
  • Final Accounts:Understand the preparation of final accounts, including the Trading, Profit & Loss Account, and Balance Sheet.
  • Depreciation Accounting:Grasp methods of calculating and recording depreciation on assets.
  • Bank Reconciliation Statement:Learn to reconcile differences between bank statements and accounting records.
  • Rectification of Errors:Understand how to identify and correct errors in financial statements.
